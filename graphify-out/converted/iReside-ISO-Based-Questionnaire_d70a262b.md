3.6.4.3 ISO-based Questionnaire
Directions: Please pick a rating for each criterion whether:
STRONGLY DISAGREE (1), DISAGREE (2), AGREE (3), or STRONGLY AGREE (4).
Note: This questionnaire is aligned with the current product quality model in ISO/IEC 25010:2023, which is the active edition used in 2025.

3.6.5 Data Categorization
The following range of weighted means and interpretations will be used to classify the responses from IT experts and end users regarding the usability, effectiveness, and overall software quality of the iReside system based on ISO/IEC 25010 quality standards.
STRONGLY AGREE (4): The respondents strongly agree that the iReside system fully satisfies the specified ISO/IEC 25010 quality characteristic, demonstrating excellent performance and a high degree of acceptability.
AGREE (3): The respondents agree that the iReside system adequately satisfies the specified ISO/IEC 25010 quality characteristic, demonstrating satisfactory performance and acceptability.
DISAGREE (2): The respondents disagree that the iReside system satisfies the specified ISO/IEC 25010 quality characteristic, indicating that improvement is needed in that aspect of the system.
STRONGLY DISAGREE (1): The respondents strongly disagree that the iReside system satisfies the specified ISO/IEC 25010 quality characteristic, indicating critical inadequacy and the need for major improvement.
