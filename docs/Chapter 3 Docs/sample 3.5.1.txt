3.5.1 Proposed System Context Diagram

Figure 16. Data Flow Diagram Level 0

The above diagram shows an overview of the features of the system. There are four primary user
roles being Administrator, Chairperson, Library Staff, and Researcher. The Administrator oversees the
system’s overall management, including user account creation, access control, and research manuscript
management. They can enable or disable accounts, upload and delete research manuscripts, and review
and approve access requests. Additionally, they have access to system analytics, system logs, and receive
system notifications.
Meanwhile, the Chairperson oversees and ensures their department’s relevant manuscripts are
visible to researchers. They can upload manuscripts to be approved by administrators, publish and
unpublish, as well as edit and delete manuscript files when necessary.
On the other hand, the Library Staff can view, enable and disable online researchers, send
warning messages, review and approve access requests as well as view notifications.
Lastly, Researchers are the primary users of the system, able to browse, search, and access articles
in the system. They also have access to saving and exporting references, making bookmarks, requesting
for manuscript access, creating and managing collaboration teams, and creating and managing personal
chatbot discourse.
